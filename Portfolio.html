The Waltham Forest College Project (WFCP)

East London Waterworks Park + Global Regeneration + Polypipe

Role: Architectural CAD Designer

The project begun with ELWP hosting a workshop as a work experience initiative. With the help of consultants and ELWP, Waltham Forest College students propossed a design, to make use of
a derelict space where students would gather at lunch time. The aim was to design a SUD garden (sustainable Urban Drainage), which aimed to enhance bio-diversity in the area and promoted envionrmental awareness into the students studies for their future careers. My responisbility was to produce Architectural CAD drawings contractors and the local council could use to disucss
it's feasibility.

Software Used:
-Rhino 8
-AutoCAD

Cultivating Resliance Project

The University of Reading

Aimed at preparing for the effects of climate change through a series of phases which develop from a bio-food lab into a safe haven for a community of people whom travel via boat.
Heavy emphasis on modular design to allow for flexible changes through the phases. This project helped me achieve a First class degree in Architecture (BSc).
